Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What we collect

NewsCore collects only the email address you voluntarily provide when signing up for the daily digest. No account registration is required to read news.

Bookmarks are stored entirely in your browser's localStorage — they never leave your device and we have no access to them.

2. How we use your email

Your email is used solely to send the daily AI news digest you subscribed to. We do not sell, share, or rent your email address to any third party.

3. Cookies & tracking

NewsCore uses a single session cookie for the admin panel. We do not use advertising cookies, third-party trackers, or analytics that identify individual users.

4. Third-party services

We use Google Gemini API to generate article summaries. Article content sent to Gemini is sourced from publicly available RSS feeds. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details.

We host on Render and use Neon PostgreSQL for data storage. Both operate under their own privacy policies.

5. Data retention

Email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe. Article data is retained indefinitely for historical browsing. You may request deletion of your email by contacting us.
